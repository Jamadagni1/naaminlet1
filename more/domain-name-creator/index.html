Domain tool is intentionally private. Naamin experts create domain options manually for brands, startups, companies, institutions and creators.

  • Brands & Startups
  • Companies & Firms

How It Works

  • Step 1: Share business/startup details and preferred style.
  • Step 2: We curate domain suggestions with naming logic.
